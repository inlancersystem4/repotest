How it works

Learn the simple steps to earning

Join or Create a Quiz

Create or browse through our wide selection of educational quizzes covering various topics and difficulty levels. Whether you're interested in science, history, literature, or math, we have something for everyone.

Place Your Bet

Once you've selected a quiz, it's time to place your bet. You can choose to bet on your own quiz or challenge a friend or another user. The higher the stakes, the greater the rewards!

Test Your Knowledge

Now it's time to put your knowledge to the test! Answer the quiz questions to the best of your ability and see how you stack up against the competition. Each correct answer brings you one step closer to victory.

Win Rewards

If you emerge victorious, you'll earn the bet of the quiz. It's a win-win situation: you learn while you earn!
